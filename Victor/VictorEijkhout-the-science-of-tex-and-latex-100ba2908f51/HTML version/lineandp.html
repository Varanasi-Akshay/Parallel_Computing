TeX course : Line and page breaking

Part of the strength of TeX is the sophistication of its algorithms for breaking lines into paragraphs, and lists of paragraphs (and tables, formulas, et cetera) into pages. These algorithms are not immediately obvious. The paragraph breaking algorithm takes us into dynamic programming; the page break algorithm will let us have a look at NP-completeness.
