TeX course : Document markup

TeX itself is almost like assembly language in the freedom that it gives the user to typeset a document in crude, possibly efficient, and often ad hoc ways. LaTeX, on the other hand, tries to effect a separation of intentions regarding structure, and their actual typeset realisation.

Here we will take a look at document markup in general, comparing TeX to older and newer systems.
